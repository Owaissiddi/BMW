The Rolex Root Beer is a beloved and iconic model with a fascinating history. The Root Beer name refers to specific Rolex GMT-Master models.The first
GMT-Master, reference 6542, was released in 1954 and featured a two-tone red and blue bezel inspired by the colors of Pan American Airways. Over
time, Rolex experimented with different bezel colors, and the Rolex Root Beer emerged and gained a dedicated following among collectors.The original
Root Beer model featured a brown dial and a bezel crafted from both gold and brown elements, earning it the soda-inspired nickname. Although Rolex
never officially adopted the Root Beer name, it has become widely recognized in the watch community.
